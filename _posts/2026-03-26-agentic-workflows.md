  <h1>Agentic Workflows</h1>
  <p>In fast-moving enterprise environments, I take ownership of complex flows and shape them into resilient, automated delivery paths. This post pulls together practical patterns from my work at major companies.</p>

  <h2>1. Design for autonomy</h2>
  <p>Build services that operate with explicit intent and minimal human intervention, while retaining observability and safety checks, especially for security-sensitive operations.</p>

  <h2>2. Implement strong feedback loops</h2>
  <p>Deploy metrics and alerts that drive fast recovery when automation misses edge cases. Own the issue from detection through resolution.</p>

  <h2>3. Embed reliability in deployment paths</h2>
  <p>From my work with Azure/AWS and architectural patterns, you should treat each workflow component as a first-class product with tests, docs, and runbooks.</p>

  <p><strong>Outcome:</strong> consistent flow behavior under variable load, faster iteration, less firefighting, and clearer accountability.</p>
